Research

As a researcher, I'm broadly interested in machine learning, computer vision, and robotics. Much of my research is about deep reinforcement learning where I have both worked on model-free (e.g. DQN) and model-based approaches (e.g. World Models). This includes deep reinforcement learning, imitation learning, transfer learning, meta learning, and (un)self-supervised learning. I believe a robot should use computer vision to see, (un)self-supervised learning of a world model to reason, and meta-learning to leverage past experience when solving new tasks. I dream of autonomous real-world robots that learn broad and general notions of intelligence from few examples on their own to solve diverse tasks in a changing world. This entails coping with unlabelled data, learning from few examples, applying past knowledge, and adapting to a changing world environment. Ultimately, my research goal is to develop robots that perceive, think, and act on a human level when solving diverse tasks from few examples across changing environments.
